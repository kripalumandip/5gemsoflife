Five gems for success

Never be over excited

If you are a type of person who becomes over excited in your life if some change is going to happen then you have to change your habit because this habit has the following disadvantages

You will feel shame if that change is a mistake. for eg. think what will happen if you go to stage in front of every one to receive an award which is to be given to a person with the name similar to you and you mistakely heard your name. Imagine the situation.
Another demerit is that every thing you posses now will be anyway snatched from you. It is for sure. Either some other people will snatch that from you or the time will snatch your body(being dead). If you are very excited about what you have then you will feel very bad when it is snatched.

Dont waste your time in the name of entertainment

Among these two only one can exist and they are either success or entertainment.

One simple rule, if you want to be successful then forget about your entertainment and if you want to enjoy your life then forget about the success. Some people can tell that we are successful and we enjoy our life as well then I would reply them as "please ask your soul that you feel really successful while enjoying the life in the form of ignorance." This is nor success, this is the way of killing yourself because no matter how successful you think you are one day time will snatch your body from you so before that its better to work hard on life rather than enjoying the life in the form of ignorance.

Never be angry for a longer time

Every one becomes angry when their wish is not fulfilled. But being angry for a longer time is super harmful for you because

When we become angry then generally we are angry with two classes. One is the living beings like human, dog, cat, etc. and another class is the non living class like I am very angry with the earthquake because it destroyed my house. Both the living and non living are the material world and if you become angry with them then your mind will go into those things and you will gain the property of those things. for eg. if i hate bad person too much then one thing is sure that i will become bad because i have been thinking about him so i am becoming like him. So never be angry or hate someone for a longer time, just finish the matter in a shorter time.

Never cry for the worldly loss

If you are the kind of person who cries for the worldly loss then it is a clear sign that you have lost the will to rise again.

The life is a battle and it will make you fall down but it is upto you either to rise again or to accepet the defeat and cry for the whole life. We plan for one thing and suddenly another new thing happens then our best move is to adjust according the situation and keep pressing our attack from that situation. And nothing to worry about in the worldly loss because one day everything will be snatched from you even your life so just focus on your effort and dont think about the result. Make a will to prepare not the will to win.

Dont expect anything from the world

As there is one famous quotation "There are no free lunches" so we must not think that someone will give us things in our life to run our life.

This thinking is totally disastrous as in the world everyone in mean from inside though they seem generous from outside. Remember one golden statement "No one in this universe can even think for the happiness of the others then how could they do things for the happiness of others." We can see countless examples in the world like everyone respects a strong and wealthy person but when he becomes weak and poor no one will look at him. This is the reality of the world. No matter we can see false etiquete in the world but the reality is everyone is selfist. Our job is to understand and realise this fact and to work hard on our own and never to expect anything from the world.
